Nike Store

Site Name

Nike Store. This name was chosen because it embodies the iconic Nike brand, renowned for its innovative sportswear and community-driven initiatives.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Nike Store website is to:

  • Showcase the latest Nike products, including apparel, footwear, and accessories.
  • Provide information on upcoming product launches and exclusive promotions.
  • Offer resources on fitness, training, and community sports events.

Scenarios

Potential questions from site visitors:

  • What are the latest Nike shoes available for running?
  • Where can I find size guides for Nike apparel?
  • What events or challenges is Nike organizing for the fitness community?

Color Schema

#111111 - Primary color for headings and accents
#FFFFFF - Background color for the site
#FF0000 - Accent color for highlights and buttons

Typography

Fonts to be used:

  • Headings: Futura, sans-serif
  • Body: Roboto, sans-serif
